💡 How It Works
01
Input Your Message
Paste any email, SMS, or text message into the detector. Supports any length of text.
02
AI Preprocessing
Text is cleaned, lowercased, stop words removed, Porter-stemmed, then converted to a 3000-feature TF-IDF vector.
03
SVM Classification
The linear-kernel SVM model (loaded from AWS S3) classifies the message with 97.2% accuracy in under 1 second.
